We are leaving in an Information Technology and Communication age and at its core is Computer Science. It is therefore without a doubt that, the study of computer science is critical for socio-economic advancement of society. As it will help take advantage of the benefits Information Technology and Communication has to offer. The Computer Science Department of the Koforidua Technical University studies both the fundamentals of computation and computationâ€™s interaction with the world. The objective of the department is to educate and train students as computer scientists to develop new algorithms, invent new systems and theories to empower people and the society, and also advance the science of computing in future as engineers, scientists, social scientists, lawyers, artists, and others around the university and beyond. Consequently the Computer Science Department of Koforidua Technical University offers courses such as Operating Systems & Open Source, Data Structures and Algorithms, System Analysis and Design, Databases, Programming languages, Software Engineering, Web Development Concepts, Distributing Computing, Operations Research, Social, Legal and Ethics Implications of Computing, Computer Networking, Information Systems and Management, Computer Security, IT Project Management, Human Computer Interaction. The Department also offers BSc programme in Computer Science in collaboration with Valley View University and BTech in IT and Telecommunication. Lecturers and Students are also involved in a number of research and initiatives across the University and the community, to help address various issues of society such as Agriculture and the establishment of Internet Community for sustainable development.
